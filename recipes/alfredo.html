Cody's Chicken Alfredo

Description

This dish is very simple but sure to be a family favorite.

After many years of trying to create the perfect alfredo dish, I finally found the answer in the recipe below

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Prego Alfredo Sauce
  • 1 lb of Linguini Noodles
  • 2 Thawed Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Cup Mozzarella Cheese
  • Minced Garlic
  • Garlic Salt
  • Nashville Hot Chicken Seasoning
  • Montreal Chicken Seasoning

Steps

  1. Season chicken with the Hot Chicken and Montreal Chicken Seasoning
  2. Cook chicken to an internal temp of 165 degrees in air fryer (about 20 min)
  3. While chicken is cooking bring water to full boil and cook linguine to al dente (about 9 min)
  4. When linguine is about done, put sauce in seperate pan along with cheese and minced garlic
  5. Put strained and cooked noodles,cut up cooked chicken, and sauce into a pan and stir
  6. Season to taste with garlic salt
